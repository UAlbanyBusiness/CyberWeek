What is it?

In the first annual Cyber Jobs Week, you will have an opportunity to network, meet with potential employers, and receive a well-deserved placement. We are not only looking to place in firms for permanent jobs but also to place our students for internships.

Who is it for?

This event is open to seniors, as well as juniors and sophomores. It is also open to MBA students with a cybersecurity track.

Our Sponsors

Cyber Jobs Week is sponsored by the Digital Forensics program at the University at Albany. This four-year undergraduate program in digital forensics has a forward-looking curriculum that helps students build the skills and experience to answer the who, what, where, when and why of cyber incidents including cyber attacks and cyber crimes.

The event is also supported by School of Business organizations: Cyber Defense Organization, Digital Forensics Association, and ISACA.
